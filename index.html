SINGAPORE SPORTS HUB

The Singapore Sports Hub is a state-of-the-art, fully integrated sports, entertainment and lifestyle hub. Located on a 35-hectare site within the city at Kallang, it features a unique cluster of world-class facilities that includes the following:

  • A 55,000-capacity National Stadium with a retractable roof and movable tiered seating.
  • A 100PLUS Promenade that encircles the National Stadium.
  • A Water Sports Centre featuring kayaking and canoeing.
  • A Splash-N-Surf facility (Kids Waterpark, Stingray and Lazy River).
  • Community facilities such as beach volleyball courts, hard courts (futsal, basketball and netball), lawn bowls, giant chess, skate park and running & cycling paths.

    THE CROWN JEWEL

    NATIONAL STADIUM

    With events from sports to concerts, the National Stadium, also known as the crown jewel of Singapore Sports Hub, is the largest dome structure in the world. It has a cooling system better than air con, rearrangable seating and a retractable roof which covers 95% of the seats and is made of cutting-edge weather-resistant material. The roof can also function as a projector screen!

    • 100 PLUS PROMENADE

    A sheltered yet open air 888m flat running track which goes around the National Stadium that is 24 hours open and free to use by all members of the public! Enjoy a scenic view as you run with refreshments readily available in vending machines located along the track! If you ever feel like giving up during your run, look to the motivational messages on the wall to spur yourself on!





    THE WATER ELEMENT

    WATER SPORTS CENTRE

    Want to try out water sports? Head down to the Water Sports Centre at the Singapore Sports Hub whereby you can kayak, canoe or even dragon boat with a beautiful view of Singapore's city skyline!

  • Due to Covid-19 safe-distancing measurements, plesae note that only groups of 8 are allowed and only 2 hours rental are currently available.
  • Patrons must be at least 7 years old and can fit in a Personal Floatation Device (PFD).
  • Patrons under 12 must be accompanied by a responsible person aged 18 or older .

    • Opening Hours:
    7.00am - 8.00pm (Monday to Sunday) / 7.00am - 10.00pm (Public Holidays)

    • SPLASH-N-SURF

    Looking to cool down from the hot, humid Singapore weather? Head right down to the Splash-n-Surf facility at Gate 22 of the 100PLUS PROMENADE which has the Kids Water Playground, Lazy River and the Stingray.

    The Kids Water Playground is a perfect oasis for your little ones to splash, swim and slide, making it a children's wonderland!

    Next, grab a float and relax in the slow and calm Lazy River , there is no better place to unwind than here!

    Lastly, if you are up for a bit for an adventure, try wave-riding at the Stingray whereby you will experience exhiliration like no other!

  • The Kids Water Playground only allows a maximum of 8 patreons every hour.
  • The Lazy River is closed till further notice.
  • The Stingray is currently limited to 5 riders per hour but each rider can have an accompanying parent/guardian during the session.

    • Opening Hours:
    8.00am - 10.00pm (Monday to Sunday)
